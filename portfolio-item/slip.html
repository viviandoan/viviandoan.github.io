Slip

Slip creates luxurious silk products dedicated to providing a better sleep experience. Their signature Slipsilk material has been developed and refined for over ten years to provide the best silk products on the market.

project details

This redesign for Slip focuses on changes to the user experience as opposed to the visual design of the site, and so we adhered to the established brand guidelines.

One of the major changes made to the site was a rearchitecture of the Global Navigation. The new design declutters the Global Nav and brings shoppable products to the top level of the navigation. Expert Testimonials, Blog Posts, and "Slip Celebrities" were merged into one larger section called "Slip Love". Being of less importance than the shoppable products, this content was moved to the Courtesy Nav.

The desktop verison of the PDP was redesigned to have all path-to-purchase content in the viewport upon load. The informational content that was previously in the viewport was moved below the fold, as to not distract from the path-to-purchase, but users can still scroll down if they're looking for more information. The mobile version anchors the "Add to Bag" CTA to the bottom of the viewport. This ensures that the user can always easily purchase this item, no matter where they are on the page.
