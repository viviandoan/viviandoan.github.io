Optix

Optix is a proposed wearable technology
Optix is a proposed wearable technology. It is a pair of smart glasses that translates text and audio, while keeping in mind an aesthetically pleasing frame at the same time. Optix was designed with the aim of encouraging more people to expand their horizons, travel to new places, and learn new languages – in pursuit of the Global community and Global citizen.
