Optix Wearables

Optix is a concept for a new wearable technology - a pair of smart glasses that can translate text and audio in real-time, as well as identify objects.

project details

This project began with a simple design challenge: design a user friendly wearable that can help ease the pain for the solo traveler.

After conducting interviews with our target market, creating personas, and mapping out the user journey, we came up with Optix Wearables. The device features a frame, camera, photo recognition, and translator software. We also designed an accompanying app.

Optix was designed with the aim of encouraging more people to expand their horizons, travel to new places, and learn new languages – in pursuit of a Global community.
